This map has the locations of 89 data centers across the Midwest and Virginia. Clicking on a data center will give information about the facility's operator and connected fiber carriers; I am able to add other information if needed, as well. By toggling off other layers, this map can also be used to quickly visualize the data centers that major fiber carriers are currently connected to. I can also add any other carriers, I just did the 9 networks that were connected with the most data centers, as well as Midwest-based carriers WIN and Bluebird. The map also features symbology with a color gradient that reflects the number of carriers that are in a particular data center.
